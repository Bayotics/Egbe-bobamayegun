Our Pillars

Our guiding principles reflect the core values that bind us together as a society, committed to fostering unity, growth, and cultural pride. These pillars define our identity and drive our mission:

Cultural Preservation

We ensure that the richness of our ancestral heritage is passed on to future generations.

Unity & Brotherhood

We thrive on the spirit of togetherness and the responsibility of uplifting one another.

Empowerment

We empower the community through education, and socio-economic initiatives.

Who we Are

We promote our heritage on global platforms, fostering respect for diversity

Bobamayegun Okunrin Akile Ijebu

We are a society dedicated to preserving, promoting, and celebrating the rich heritage of the Ijebu people. Our mission is to foster unity, cultural pride, and community empowerment through the preservation of traditions, language, and values.

We provide a platform for cultural exchange, education, and mentorship, enabling members to connect with their roots while contributing to the development of the larger society. our identity is defined by these virtues

  • Cultural Preservation.
  • Unity & Brotherhood.
  • Empowerment.
  • Leadership.
  • Cultural Advocacy.
  • Integrity.

Why join us?

Become a Part of the Bobamayegun Family

You become part of a family dedicated to preserving and sharing the values and customs that define who we are.

Cultural Preservation

You’ll play an active role in keeping the Ijebu and Yoruba culture alive, from traditional ceremonies to language promotion.

Community Support

Our society is built on the foundation of mutual support. We believe in helping one another through life’s challenges, offering guidance, mentorship, and assistance in times of need.

Networking and Opportunities

Being part of our society connects you with individuals from various walks of life, both locally and globally
