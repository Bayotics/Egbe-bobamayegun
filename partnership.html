Corporate Partnership

At Bobamayegun Okunrin Akile Ijebu, we believe that collaboration with visionary corporate partners can enhance our impact and bring valuable support to initiatives that benefit the Ijebu community and the larger society. Our partnerships are rooted in mutual respect, shared goals, and a commitment to creating lasting, positive change.

By partnering with us, your organization joins a respected society committed to cultural preservation and promotion. You’ll be aligned with a movement that values diversity and celebrates the unique traditions of the Ijebu people, creating meaningful engagement and contributing to a more inclusive, culturally aware society.

Also, we offer a platform for corporations to engage in impactful Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) projects within Nigeria. Our programs in cultural preservation, education, youth empowerment, and environmental sustainability allow our partners to fulfill CSR goals in ways that resonate with their values and business ethos. Collaborating with us showcases your commitment to social responsibility, builds goodwill, and strengthens your corporate reputation.

Opportunities for Corporate Partnership

Bobamayegun Okunrin Akile Ijebu offers various partnership opportunities that can be tailored to your organization’s unique goals and interests:

1. We invite corporate partners to work with us on co-branded campaigns that raise awareness for cultural preservation, social issues, and community initiatives. By partnering on awareness campaigns, you’ll help amplify important messages while reaching a broad audience that values culture and community support.

2. Positive brand positioning and awareness among the Ijebu diaspora and wider public.

3. If you have a specific partnership vision in mind, we would be delighted to collaborate and create a unique program tailored to align with your organization’s CSR goals and the needs of our community.

As a corporate partner of Bobamayegun Okunrin Akile Ijebu, you can expect transparency, professionalism, and a strong commitment to achieving mutually beneficial goals. We offer regular updates on the impact of your contributions, provide data and feedback on programs and events, and ensure that your brand is represented respectfully and prominently. We also welcome corporate donations from those companies who believe in our mission and wish to support our core work. Contact Asiwaju Adeniyi, Corporate Relations Manager, at testbobamayegun@gmail.com for more info.

Events Partnership

Join us as a sponsor of our cultural events, such as the Ojude Oba Cultural Festival, Youth Empowerment Workshops, and Annual Heritage Celebrations. Sponsoring our events offers your brand visibility and engagement with our members, participants, and attendees, providing a platform for experiential marketing. You’ll also gain access to branding opportunities through event materials, media coverage, and social media promotions.

Support specific programs that align with your organization’s objectives. This may include educational scholarships, youth mentorship programs, environmental initiatives, and health and wellness campaigns. Each program sponsorship can be customized to reflect your brand’s mission while addressing critical community needs.

If you would like to pitch an event or explore wider partnerships for either of these major cultural events, please get in touch with Abiona Wasiu, Head of Fundraising and Operations, testbobamayegun@gmail.com

Media and Marketing Partnership

There are a range of opportunities for your company or organisation to reach our wide and diverse audiences through our bespoke marketing partnerships and paid advertising opportunities. We now reach just under two million people globally per year through our events, festivals, publications, online platforms and social media channels. Our Audiences We have over 150,000 followers on social media across our different channels. An average of 10,000 people attend our events and festivals each year in Nigeria. Our audiences are diverse with a strong diaspora focus. Our audiences reflect the country’s youthful demographic with half under the age of 50. Our audiences are rounded with an interest in politics, business, arts, and education. Our audiences are connected across multiple continents

We have a number of advertising packages for Nigerian businesses on our media outlets, our leading platform for news, investigation, and opinion, which reaches over one million people globally every year.
